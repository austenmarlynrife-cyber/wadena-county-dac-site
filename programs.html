Programs & Services

Wadena County DAC offers person-centered day training, habilitation, community participation, and supportive services designed to help adults build skills, confidence, and meaningful routines.

Start intake form Contact our team

Services & Activities

Our program blends structure, community engagement, and individualized support.

  • Daily activity planning that supports independence and routine
  • Life-skills practice, communication, and social connection
  • Community outings, volunteering, and inclusive participation
  • Creative activities such as crafts, music, games, and seasonal events

Eligibility / Referrals

If you are not sure where to begin, we can help walk you through the process.

  • Referrals may come from families, guardians, case managers, or county staff
  • We can help review current supports and discuss program fit
  • Our intake form helps us understand needs, goals, and contact information
  • We follow up directly to discuss next steps, scheduling, and documentation

Transportation

Transportation options vary based on need, location, and available supports.

  • We can discuss what transportation options may be available
  • Families and case managers can include transportation needs on the intake form
  • We will help identify practical next steps and local coordination options

What to Expect

After you submit an intake, our director or staff team will review it and reach out.

  • Review of participant needs, goals, and support information
  • Follow-up call or email from the DAC team
  • Discussion of program fit, schedule, and services
  • Guidance on next paperwork or planning steps
