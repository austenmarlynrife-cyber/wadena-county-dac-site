Programs & Services

Daily programming, community activities, skill-building, and supportive services designed to help people stay connected and thrive.

Services & Activities
We offer structured daily programming that supports independence, connection, and confidence. Activities may include crafts, music, community outings, life skills, volunteering, and opportunities to build routines that work for each person.
Eligibility / Referrals
If you’re unsure where to start, we can help. Contact us and we’ll walk you through eligibility and referral steps, including how to coordinate with county or case management support.
Transportation
Transportation options vary. If transportation is a need, reach out and we’ll help clarify what’s available and what alternatives might work for your situation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide person-centered day training and habilitation that helps adults with disabilities build life skills, strengthen independence, and participate fully in community life. At Wadena County DAC, we create meaningful daily opportunities through supportive programming, community connection, and practical services that honor each person’s goals, choices, and dignity.
