Treasure Trove Thrift Store

A local favorite for unique finds and affordable everyday items.

Treasure Trove is more than a thrift store. It helps support services at Wadena County DAC while giving the community a fun place to shop, donate, and discover something new each week.

Donate to the Store

Clean, gently used donations are welcome and appreciated.

  • Clothing and shoes in good condition
  • Household goods and kitchen items
  • Home decor and small furniture
  • Seasonal and holiday items

If you are not sure whether an item is a fit, call us and we will gladly help.
